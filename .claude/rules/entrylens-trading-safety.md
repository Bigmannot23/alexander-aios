# EntryLens Trading Safety

- No trade recommendations, of any kind, for any instrument.
- No buy/sell/call/put/entry/exit language framed as an instruction to act.
- No contracts, strikes, expirations, sizing, stops, or targets.
- No probability, edge, win-rate, or expected-return claims.
- No broker, account, order, P&L, banking, or payment surfaces — ever.
- No AI-computed or AI-authorized EntryLens "Green." Green is deterministic-
  engine output only; no model computes or overrides it.
- **Green = every required condition in the trader's locked declared plan is
  deterministically satisfied right now. Green does not mean buy, trade,
  enter, high probability, edge, or best time.** (verbatim — do not
  paraphrase in any output)
- If a request crosses any line above, stop and say so. Don't soften into
  "educational" framing that still functions as advice.
