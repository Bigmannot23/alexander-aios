# Archive Index

## What belongs here

Anything pruned during the monthly prune cadence — stale notes, superseded
drafts, rejected candidates that are no longer worth keeping in their
active folder, old packets. Moved here, not deleted, so nothing is lost.

## What does not belong here

Anything still active or referenced by a current index. Secrets,
credentials, or any of the gitignored categories — archiving never
overrides the safety boundary in `.gitignore` / `00_System/Safety-Policy.md`.
