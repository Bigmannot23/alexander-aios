# Research Library Index

## What belongs here

General research staging that doesn't belong to a specific system folder —
e.g. cross-cutting market-structure research, tooling research, or
anything still being scoped before it's clear whether it's an AlphaLab,
EntryLens, or Productization Lab concern.

## What does not belong here

Anything that already has an obvious home — AlphaLab-specific research
goes in `04_AlphaLab/`, course material in `05_Course-Library/_index.md`,
YouTube sources in `06_YouTube-Lesson-Library/`. This folder is for
research that doesn't fit those yet, not a dumping ground once it does.
