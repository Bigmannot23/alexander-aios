# AlphaLab Mistake Taxonomy Index

## What belongs here

A catalog of repeated mistakes mined from post-session reviews, each with
evidence files, trigger conditions, a proposed *process control* (never
advice), scenario-test implications, and whether it's an EntryLens
promotion candidate.

## What does not belong here

One-off mistakes that haven't repeated. Judgments about a trader's
skill or character — this is a process-control catalog, not a scorecard.
