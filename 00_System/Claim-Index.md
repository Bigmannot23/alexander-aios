# Claim Index

## What belongs here

One row per audited claim, repo-wide — any factual, legal, financial, or
performance claim that entered a lesson, packet, or promotion candidate.
Each row links to the full claim-audit (written with
`Templates/claim-audit.md`) wherever it actually lives (alongside its
source lesson/packet).

## What does not belong here

The full audit text. This is a ledger, not the audits themselves.
Unaudited claims do not get a row — if a claim hasn't been audited yet, it
hasn't been promoted yet (`Promotion-Rules.md`).
