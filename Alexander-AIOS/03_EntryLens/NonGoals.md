# EntryLens — Non-Goals

## What belongs here

An explicit, restated list of what EntryLens is **not** — restated here
because false Green is the highest-risk product failure in the entire
stack, and that risk justifies redundant statement.

## What does not belong here

Anything aspirational about what EntryLens could become. This file is a
boundary, not a roadmap.

## Read-first file

This file, then `Product-Brief.md`.

## Source-of-truth files

`Master-Blueprint-V1.md` §1.5.

---

## EntryLens is NOT

A scanner, signal generator, buy/sell recommender, high-probability setup
finder, broker execution layer, contract picker, exit manager, trading
dashboard, market predictor, generalized trading coach, journal-first
product, auto-trader, or live AI trading assistant.

**False Green is the highest-risk product failure in the entire stack.**

## Restated boundary (see also `Product-Brief.md`)

> **Green = every required condition in the trader's locked declared plan
> is deterministically satisfied right now.**
> **Green does not mean buy, trade, enter, high probability, edge, or best
> time.**

## Rejected alternatives (never reconsider without a new decision log)

A live Claude trading bot; a live trade caller; an AI chart reader
authorizing Green; a scanner/signal generator; a dashboard-first EntryLens;
EntryLens + AlphaLab product code sharing one repo. See
`Master-Blueprint-V1.md` §14 for the full rejected-alternatives list.
