# EntryLens Predicate Candidates Index

## What belongs here

Deterministic predicate *candidates* only — staged, not wired into any
engine. A candidate is a precisely defined, observable, falsifiable
condition (e.g. "completed-bar confirmation gate") proposed for eventual
EntryLens use. Nothing here executes or evaluates anything live.

## What does not belong here

Runtime code. Anything non-deterministic, probabilistic, or advisory.
Anything that hasn't gone through `04_AlphaLab/Predicate-Candidates/_index.md`
and the promotion packet process first if it originated in AlphaLab — see
`00_System/Promotion-Rules.md`. Direct edits to a separate `entrylens/`
engine repo never happen from here.
