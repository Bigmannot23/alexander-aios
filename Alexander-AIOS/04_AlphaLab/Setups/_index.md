# AlphaLab Setups Index

## What belongs here

hypotheses for historical review, never as live recommendations. Per the
blueprint's claim-audit transformation table: "Take calls when X" becomes
"setup concept + historical-review hypothesis."

## What does not belong here

Anything telling a reader to take a specific action now. Win-rate or edge
claims. Contract/sizing guidance. Live commentary.
