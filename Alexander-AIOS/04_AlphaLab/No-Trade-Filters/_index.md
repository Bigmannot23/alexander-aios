# AlphaLab No-Trade-Filters Index

## What belongs here

Avoid-condition *candidates* — observable conditions under which a trader's
own plan says to stand down. Per the blueprint's claim-audit transformation
table: "Avoid chop" becomes "no-trade filter candidate with observable
conditions." These are exactly the kind of artifact that can later become
an EntryLens Orange/Gray reason code, via the promotion lock.

## What does not belong here

Vague advice ("avoid bad setups"). Anything that isn't an observable,
checkable condition. Direct edits to EntryLens reason codes — those only
happen through `00_System/Promotion-Rules.md`.
