# AlphaLab Review Rubrics Index

## What belongs here

Rubrics used to review a completed trading session against the trader's
own declared plan — discipline/process-adherence scoring only, run
post-session.

## What does not belong here

Rubrics that score trade quality, edge, or outcome probability. This is a
discipline mirror, not a performance grade on the market call itself.
